Watch out for cars.

小心车。

Don't walk on the road.

不要走在车道上。

Always cross at crosswalks.

一定要走斑马线。

Be careful when you cross the street.

你过马路时，要小心。

Look sideways while you are walking.

走的时候要看两边。

Watch for the traffic lights.

要看红绿灯。

Make sure you walk when the light is green.

确定是绿灯时，你才能走。

Raise your hand when you cross the street.

过马路时要举起手。

Don't play with your cell phone on the street.

不要在街上玩手机。

Don't run. Walk slowly.

不要跑，慢慢走。

Don't rush.

不要赶。

Go straight to school.

直接去学校。

Don't ever follow strangers.

绝对不可以跟陌生人走。

Go along the broad street.

走大路。

Don't go down the narrow back street.

不要走狭窄偏僻的街道。

Listen to your teacher.

要听老师的话。

Ask your teacher if you want to go to the bathroom.

如果你要去厕所，你要跟老师说一声。

Be nice to your friends.

对朋友要友善。

Turn off your cell phone at school.

在学校时要关掉手机。

Eat plenty for lunch.

午餐多吃点。

Don't stop at the game machine. Just pass by it.

不要在游戏机前停留，走过去就好。

What should you do when there is a bully?

当遇到人欺负你的时候你该怎么做？

If you meet a bully, tell me at once.

如果你被欺负，要立刻告诉我。
