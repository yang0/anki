Don't talk to strangers.

不要跟陌生人说话。

Don't follow anyone even if they ask.

即使有人让你跟他走，你也不可以跟他走。

Don't tell a stranger your name or number.

不要告诉陌生人你的姓名和电话。

Tell your teacher or mom if something weird happens.

如果发生奇怪的事要告诉老师或妈妈。

Ask for help when a stranger comes.

有陌生人出现时要请求别人帮忙。

Don't go near a stranger's car.

不要靠近陌生人的车子。

Don't get in a stranger's car.

不要上陌生人的车。

Just say no firmly.

坚决地说不。

Only walk on busy streets.

你要在繁忙的街道上走。

Walk where people can see you.

走在有人可以看到你的地方。

Just run if something feels wrong.

觉得不对劲就跑。
