Let's play with clay.

我们来玩黏土吧。

Get out the colored clay.

拿出彩色黏土。

Rub it for a while so it will mix well.

搓揉一段时间，让它混合得更好。

Which shape do you want to make? /What do you want to make?

你想做出什么形状呢？/你想做一个什么呢？

I am going to make a person.

我要捏一个人。

The leg keeps coming off/bending.

腿一直掉下来/弯掉。

Press it firmly with your hands.

拿手用力按。

It's good to use a carving knife for this.

这时候最好用刻刀。

The clay lump is too big.

这一块黏土太大了。

Let's make it into a round shape.

我们来把它捏成圆形。

Put a lump between your palms and roll it.

放一块在两手中间，然后揉搓它。

Try stretching it out.

试着把它拉开。

Let's spread it with a roller.

用滚筒把它压开。

Make the eyes, nose and mouth, and attach them on the face.

做好眼睛、鼻子、嘴巴后，把它们贴在脸上。

You can use the molds to have different shapes.

你可以使用模具做出不同的形状。

Don't press it too hard.

不要按得太用力。

Your hands are all muddy.

你满手都是泥。

Be careful not to get the mud on your clothes.

小心不要把泥沾到衣服上。

It got hardened already.

已经变硬了。

We need to store the leftovers in an airtight bag.

我们必须把剩余的黏土装进密封袋内。
