Do you want to ride a bike?

你想要骑脚踏车吗？

Let's learn how to ride a bike with two wheels.

我们来学习怎样骑两轮的脚踏车吧。

Let's take off the training wheels.

我们把辅助车轮拿掉吧。

Can you hold the bike so that I won't fall?

你可以抓住脚踏车别让我摔倒吗？

Promise you won't let go of it.

答应我你不会放手。

Don't let go of my bike, mommy.

妈妈，不可以放开脚踏车。

The bike keeps falling.

脚踏车一直倒。

Don't be nervous.

不用紧张。

Grab the handlebars tightly.

抓紧把手。

Make sure you put on your helmet.

确定你戴了头盔。

Turn the handle toward the left/right.

把把手往左/右转。

Step on the pedal hard.

用力踩踏板。

Turn the pedal backward a little and then step forward.

把踏板往后转一点点，再往前踩。

Keep an eye on the road ahead.

要看着前面的路。

Watch out for cars coming.

小心车子出现。

Be careful going downhill.

下坡时要小心。

Hold the brakes.

抓住刹车。

Don't ride your bike on the road.

不可以在车道上骑脚踏车。

It's too fast. Hold down the brake lever slightly.

太快了，轻轻抓住刹车握把。

Don't grab the brake levers suddenly while riding fast. You could fall.

当你骑很快的时候，不要突然抓住刹车握把。你会摔倒。

See how fast I'm going?

看到我骑得多快了吗？

Here we have a great bike rider!

我们这里有个很棒的单车骑士！

Wow, you are a great rider.

哇，你是个很棒的单车骑士。

Don't forget to put the lock on.

不要忘记上锁。
