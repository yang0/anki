Lesson 3 My Family

第三课 我的家庭

My father is a teacher. He works during the day.

我爸爸是老师。他白天上班。

My mother is a nurse. She works at night.

妈妈则是护士。她晚上上班。

They only see each other on the weekends.

他们只有在周末才会碰面。

My brothers, my sister and I don't work.

哥哥、妹妹和我没有上班。

We are students.

我们都是学生。

Because my parents have to work, we do the housework.

因为爸妈要上班，家务都由我们来做。

But we never complain.

但我们从不埋怨。
