Lesson 17 My Hometown

第十七课 我的家乡

My hometown is not very modern, but it's beautiful.

我的家乡不很现代化，但它却很美。

There is a big park in the center of my hometown.

家乡的中心位置有一座大公园。

There is also a swimming pool there. It is next to the park.

那儿也有一座游泳池。它紧邻公园。

My school is across from the swimming pool.

我的学校就在游泳池的对面。

They are all near my home.

它们离我家都很近。

In fact, there is no place like my hometown.

事实上，没有一个地方像我的家乡一样。
