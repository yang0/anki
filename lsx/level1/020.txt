Lesson 20 She Is Beautiful

第二十课 她很漂亮

Wow! Who is that lady next to Bob? She's pretty.

哇！鲍勃身旁的那位小姐是谁呀？她好漂亮。

Do you really think so?

你真地这么认为吗？

Sure. Look at her lovely blond hair and blue eyes.

当然。瞧瞧她那漂亮的金发和蓝眼睛。

You're right. She is beautiful.

你说对了。她很美。

Well, who is she?

那么，她是谁呢？

She's my mother.

她是我妈妈。
