Lesson 25 My Father

第二十五课　我的父亲

My father does the same thing every morning.

我父亲每天早上都做相同的事。

He gets up at six o'clock.

他六点起床。

He washes, brushes his teeth and combs his hair.

他洗脸、刷牙、梳头发。

Then, he puts on his clothes and eats breakfast.

然后他就穿衣吃早饭。

At six-thirty he listens to the news on the radio.

他六点半听收音机播报的新闻。

At exactly seven o'clock he leaves the house.

他七点整出门。

My father has very regular habits.

我父亲有很规律的习惯。
