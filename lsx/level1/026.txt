Lesson 26 He Usually Plays Tennis

第二十六课　他经常打网球

What do you usually do on Sundays?

你星期天通常做些什么事？

I usually go for a walk in the mountains.

我通常到山中走一走。

Does your husband go with you?

你先生跟你一块儿去吗？

No. He doesn't.

不。他不去。

What does he do?

他做什么呢？

He usually plays tennis.

他通常打网球。
