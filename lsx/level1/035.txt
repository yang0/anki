Lesson 35 There's Little Food

第三十五课　菜很少

Fred's having a party, but he's very embarrassed.

弗雷德在开宴会，但是他觉得很不好意思。

There are many people at his party, but there's very little food.

赴宴的人很多，不过菜却很少。

There's very little meat. There isn't much rice, either.

肉很少,饭也不够多。

There are only a few cans of coke and a little orange juice.

只有几罐可乐和一些橙汁。

However, everyone is happy at the party because there's plenty of beer.

不过，因为啤酒很多，所以宴会上每个人都很高兴。
