Lesson 51 My Girlfriend

第五十一课 我的女友

My girlfriend always complains.

我的女友老爱发牢骚。

According to her, I should be more ambitious.

根据她的说法，我应当更有进取心。

I should work harder and earn more money.

我应当更卖力地工作，挣更多的钱。

She also thinks I should live in a bigger and nicer apartment.

她也认为我应当住更大更好的公寓。

In a way, she's right.

就某方面来说，她的话是对的。

However, she should be more considerate and less critical.

不过，她也应当多体谅少批评。

After all, I am trying my best.

毕竟，我正尽力而为。

Besides, money isn't everything.

而且，金钱并非万能呀。
