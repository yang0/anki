Lesson 59 My Best Friend My best friend is the most thoughtful person in the world.

第五十九课　我最好的朋友：我最好的朋友是世界上最体贴的人。

When I'm sad, he is the most sympathetic.

当我难过时，他最富有同情心了。

When I'm sick, he is the most concerned.

我生病时，他最为关切。

When I'm in trouble, he is the most helpful.

我有困难时，他最帮我了。

In fact, he is the most dependable person in the world.

事实上，他是世界上最值得信赖的人。

He is the closest person to me.

他是我最亲的人。

He is my father.

他就是我父亲。
