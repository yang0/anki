Lesson 67 Getting Married: My girlfriend and I are going to get married this year.

第六十七课 结婚: 我和女友今年将结婚。

We are going to have a big wedding party.

我们将举行盛大的婚宴。

All our friends and relatives are going to come.

我们所有的亲朋好友都会来参加。

We are going to have a traditional wedding.

我们将要举行传统的婚礼。

After the wedding we are going to live with my parents.

婚礼后我们要和我的父母亲一起住。

We are going to be one big happy family.

我们将成为一个快乐的大家庭。
