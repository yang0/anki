Lesson 68 Are You Going to Come: Are you going to come to my wedding?

第六十八课 你会来吗: 你会来参加我的婚礼吗？

Of course.

乔:当然。

Good. Are you going to bring your girlfriend?

凯蒂:太好了。你会带女朋友一起来吗？

Yes. She likes weddings.

乔:会的。她喜欢参加婚礼。

So when are you going to get married?

凯蒂:那么，你们什么时候要结婚呢？

We haven't decided yet.

乔:我们尚未决定。
