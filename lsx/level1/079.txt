Lesson 79 Showing Off. Many people are fond of showing off.

第七十九课 炫耀 有很多人喜欢炫耀。

They always insist on footing the bill when they are out with friends.

当他们和朋友外出时总是争着付帐。

They always think of impressing people with their wealth.

他们老是想用自己的财富给别人留下深刻的印象。

They seem to think that money can buy friends.

他们似乎认为钱可以买到朋友。

In fact, it can't.

但事实上是不可能的。

Instead of trying to show off, they should be themselves.

他们不应炫耀，而应做真正的自我。
