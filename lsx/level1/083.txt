Lesson 83 A Nice Person Shelly doesn't like people who are unreliable.

大好人 谢莉不喜欢不可靠的人。

She especially doesn't like people who tell lies. On the other hand, she likes people who are humorous and honest.

她特别不喜欢说谎的人。另一方面，她喜欢那些幽默又诚实的人。

She really likes people that are easygoing and sincere. However, regardless of whom she likes or dislikes, she is nice to everyone.

她实在喜欢随和又诚恳的人。然而，不论她喜欢谁或不喜欢谁，她对每个人都很好。
