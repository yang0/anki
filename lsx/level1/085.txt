Lesson 85 Blood Types Some people believe that one's personal- ity is related to one's blood type.

血型 有些人相信一个人的个性与他的血型有关。

There are three main blood types: A, B and O. Type A people are introverts whereas type 0 people are extroverts.

一共有三种主要 的血型：A型、B型及O型。A型人内向而O型人则外向。

Type B people are a mixture of both A and O. They are more relaxed and don't get bothered easily.

B型人是A型和O型的混合。他们比较轻松自在而且不容易觉得烦。

谈到择偶的时候，切记以上这些事实。
