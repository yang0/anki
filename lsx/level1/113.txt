Lesson 113 Something Well Begun Is Half Done Work is a very important part of life.

第113课　好的开始是成功的一半 工作是生活中非常重要的一部分。

People work in order to earn a living. A country can only make progress if its people work.

人们为了谋生而工作。一个国家只有在其人民工作下才会进步。

It is, therefore, important for us to be hard-working. When we work, there is one golden rule we should always remember:

因此，努力工作对我们很重要。在工作时，有一则我们必须牢记的金科玉律就是：

Something well begun is half done.

好的开始是成功的一半。
